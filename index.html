Paper Writing Tracker

A drag-and-drop planner for multi-author papers. Assignments live at the subsection level (bold rows) and workload is counted there; sub-subsections keep their own status underneath. Grab the ⠿ handle to reorder or move items; double-click any title to rename; hover for ✎ / ✕; use the faint “＋ add …” lines to create rows. Nothing leaves your browser — use Export to share a plan file.

Import from LaTeX

Paste your paper's LaTeX source. The tracker builds the \section → \subsection → \subsubsection hierarchy, reads owners from \TODO{Name} / \TODO{Name + Name} tags (a tag on a subsubsection rolls up into its subsection's chips; ?? or empty → NEEDS OWNER), estimates status from how much prose each part contains, and flags XX placeholder cells in tables. Any owners you assigned by hand are kept wherever the source still says ??. Re-importing rebuilds the structure and order from the source.
